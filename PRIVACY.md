# PagePins Privacy Policy

PagePins stores the checklists ("pins") you create in `chrome.storage.sync`, inside
your own Chrome profile. If you are signed into Chrome with sync enabled, Chrome
synchronizes that data across your devices through your own Google account — the same
mechanism Chrome uses for bookmarks.

- **No data leaves your browser to us.** PagePins has no servers, makes no network
  requests, and includes no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
- **The developer never sees your data.** Pin contents, page URLs, and settings are
  readable only by your Chrome profile.
- **Page access** (`http://*/*`, `https://*/*`) exists solely to draw the pin bar on
  the pages you visit and to key pins to the page URL. Page content is never read,
  collected, or transmitted.
- **Deleting data:** remove items or use *Clear all pins* (per page), the options
  page's *Clear all pins* (everything), or uninstall the extension.

Questions or concerns: open an issue at
[github.com/turkycat/PagePins](https://github.com/turkycat/PagePins/issues).
